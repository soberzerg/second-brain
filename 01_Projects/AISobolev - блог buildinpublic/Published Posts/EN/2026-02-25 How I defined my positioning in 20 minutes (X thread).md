# How I defined my positioning in 20 minutes (X thread)

The usual process:

Someone asks "what do you do?"
You freeze.
You stare at the ceiling.
You squeeze out "I help businesses with AI."

Sounds like everyone else.

Tweet 3:
What I did differently:

I built a personal knowledge base. All my projects. Clients. Case studies. Decisions. Numbers.

Not a notes app. A system AI can actually read.

Tweet 4:
When I loaded a positioning prompt, AI didn't start from zero.

It already knew:

"What situations do clients come to you with?"
"What do they thank you for? Specifically."
"What do you do that others don't?"

Questions a $1000 consultant would ask. But sharper.

AI without your context = generic fluff.

Same tool. Completely different output.
