# 100 Questions for Customer Development Interviews (B2C)

## Customer Segmentation

| # | Question | Goal | Notes |
|---|----------|------|-------|
| 1 | What do you do professionally? | Find out more about the customer | |
| 2 | Who handles the process (you're improving) at home/at the office? | Find out more about the context | |
| 3 | Tell me more about your role at the company | Find out more about the context | |
| 4 | Specific relevant questions related to your customer (i.e., do you have kids) | Find out more about the customer | |
| 5 | How much time do you spend on (process you're improving)? | Find out more about the context | This helps you scale the problem |
| 6 | How much money does (the problem) cost you? | Find out more about the context | This helps you scale the problem |
