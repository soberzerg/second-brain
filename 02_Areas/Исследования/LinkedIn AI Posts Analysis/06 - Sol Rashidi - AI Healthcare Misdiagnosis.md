## Текст поста (реконструкция на основе множественных источников)

Пост начинается с истории о конкретном кейсе в healthcare-компании, где AI-диагностическая система дала неверный диагноз, который чуть не привёл к вредному лечению.

Ключевые тезисы:

- AI gave a clear diagnosis. The doctor trusted it.
- But the AI was wrong — it nearly led to harmful treatment
- This was a near-miss that could have had devastating consequences

Sol подчёркивает критический принцип: AI must support experts, not replace them.

Из связанной статьи Business Insider (Sol Rashidi worked in AI at AWS and IBM):

"I use six to eight AI tools every day. But when using the tools, I always ask myself: Am I using this to accelerate work I have to do, or am I using it to do the work for me?"

"I told my junior scientists: I'm paying for your brain and uniqueness. I'm not paying you to copy and paste, because a license for enterprise API from OpenAI is a lot cheaper than you."

"If you don't use critical thinking and depend on yourself to solve problems, you could be outdated within a few years."
