## Текст поста (реконструкция)

We tried replacing 1,000 human jobs with AI.

The results were shocking — and not for the reasons you'd think.

That's our proxy for an Economic AGI benchmark.

Ryan Brandt and I scraped over 1,000 latest job postings and used the latest AI models (o1, Claude, Gemini) and agents/tools (Windsurf, Axiom, etc.) to apply for jobs and attempt to complete tasks.

[Ключевые выводы из Substack-статьи:]

- Out of 1,000+ jobs tested, only ~15% were solvable by AI
- Even for solvable tasks, AI earned $0 in revenue
- Barriers included marketplace friction, market saturation, and platforms not designed for AI-driven work
- The biggest surprise wasn't what AI could do — it was what prevented it from doing it

The experiment showed that the bottleneck isn't AI capability — it's the systems, processes, and platforms that weren't built for AI workers.
