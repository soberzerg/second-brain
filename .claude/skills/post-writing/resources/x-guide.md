## Platform Overview

- **Limit:** 280 characters per tweet, threads for long-form
- **Language:** English
- **Role in strategy:** International audience, bootstrapper community, building authority in EN space
- **Voice:** "Builder thinking out loud" ‚Äî same as Russian content, but more concise and direct

---

## Formatting Rules

- **280 characters max** per single tweet ‚Äî every word must earn its place
- **Threads:** hook tweet ‚Üí 3-7 value tweets ‚Üí summary/CTA tweet
- **No markdown** ‚Äî plain text only (bold/italic don't work)
- **Images/screenshots** boost engagement significantly
- **Quote tweets** > retweets (add your take, don't just share)
- **Line breaks** matter ‚Äî use them to create visual rhythm

---

## Voice in English

The same "builder thinking out loud" voice, translated to English:

**Do:**
- Direct, punchy sentences
- First person ("I", "my", "we")
- Specific numbers ("$2000 MRR" not "good revenue")
- Contrarian takes backed by experience
- Show the work, not just the result

**Don't:**
- Formal business English ("I would like to share...")
- Motivational fluff ("Believe in yourself!")
- Translate Russian posts word-for-word (rewrite, don't translate)
- Use buzzwords without substance ("leverage synergies")

## Thread Structure Formula

```
Tweet 1: [Hook ‚Äî bold claim or surprising data] + "Thread üßµ"
Tweet 2-N: [One point per tweet, each tweet standalone readable]
Final tweet: [Summary + follow CTA]
```

**Rules:**
- Every tweet should be readable standalone (someone might see just one in their feed)
- Use ‚Üí arrows for lists within tweets
- Number your points if it's a list thread ("1/", "2/", etc.)
- Keep each tweet under 250 chars for better readability
- Add a relevant image/screenshot to tweet 1 when possible

## Engagement Tactics

- **Quote tweets** ‚Äî add your take to others' content (builds connections)
- **Reply to bigger accounts** ‚Äî thoughtful replies get noticed
- **Post consistently** ‚Äî 3-5 tweets/day, 1-2 threads/week
- **Best times:** 9am, 12pm, 5pm EST (US audience)
- **Pin your best thread** ‚Äî it's your landing page
- **Use "Follow for more"** in thread CTAs ‚Äî it works

---

## What NOT to Do

- ‚ùå **Word-for-word translation** from Russian ‚Äî rewrite for EN audience
- ‚ùå **Too formal** ‚Äî "I would like to share my professional experience" sounds corporate
- ‚ùå **Thread without a hook** ‚Äî if tweet 1 doesn't grab, nobody reads tweet 2
- ‚ùå **Only self-promotion** ‚Äî give value first, sell later
- ‚ùå **Ignore replies** ‚Äî engagement is a two-way street
- ‚ùå **Too many hashtags** ‚Äî on X, 0-2 hashtags max (unlike Threads)

## Thread Checklist

- [ ] **Hook in tweet 1?** (would YOU click "show more"?)
- [ ] **Each tweet standalone?** (readable without context)
- [ ] **Under 250 chars per tweet?** (leaves room for engagement)
- [ ] **Specific numbers?** (not "a lot" but "$2000")
- [ ] **Thread delivers on hook's promise?** (no bait-and-switch)
- [ ] **CTA in final tweet?** (follow, reply, bookmark)
- [ ] **Sounds like a person?** (not a marketing bot)
- [ ] **Would you bookmark this?** (the ultimate quality test)
